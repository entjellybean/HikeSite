Cedar Forest Loop Easy 4.6

Cedar State Park

Distance: 4.2 miles

A peaceful walk through ancient cedar forests with abundant wildlife. The Cedar Forest Loop is ideal for a relaxing nature walk, suitable for families and beginners. Expect to see birds, wildflowers, and shaded paths.

Départ : Cedar State Park, Trailhead Parking Lot
